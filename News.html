Looking for Help

Seeking Management Positions

Dear Friends, Down Two Earth Esports Seeks New Management Positions Down Two Earth Esports, a leading esports organization, is looking to fill several new management positions. These positions include Discord Managers, Content Managers, and high-level positions. Discord Managers will be responsible for managing the organization’s Discord server.

This includes moderating conversations, responding to inquiries, and ensuring that the server remains a safe and welcoming environment for all members. Content Managers will be responsible for creating content for the organization’s website and social media accounts.

This includes writing articles, creating videos, and managing the organization’s presence on various platforms. High-level positions will involve working with the organization’s leadership team to develop strategies and plans for growth. This includes developing marketing campaigns, managing sponsorships, and overseeing operations. The ideal candidates should have experience in esports or related fields such as marketing or communications. Owner of Down Two Earth

Announcing Our Owner

Dear Friends, We are excited to announce that Ryan Shazier, former NFL linebacker has become the owner of Down Two Earth Esports. This is a major milestone for both Ryan and Down Two Earth Esports

as they look to make their mark in the world of competitive gaming. For those who may not be familiar with Ryan Shazier, he was a standout linebacker for the Pittsburgh Steelers from 2014-2017.

During his time with the Steelers, he was named to two Pro Bowls and was an integral part of the team’s success. Unfortunately, his career was cut short due to a spinal injury suffered in 2017. Despite this setback, Ryan has remained active in the sports world and is now looking to make his mark in esports. Down Two Earth Esports is an organization dedicated to providing competitive gaming opportunities for players of all skill levels. They have teams competing in various games such as Rocket League, CS:GO, and Valorant. With Ryan’s involvement as an owner, Down Two Earth Esports looks to expand their reach even further and become one of the top esports organizations in the world. We are excited to see what Ryan and Down Two Earth Esports can accomplish together! Be sure to follow them on social media for updates on their progress! Sincerely, Down Two Earth Esports
